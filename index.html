Formal Khaki Attire

Ladies

Formal dress, gown, or elegant long dress in khaki, pale khaki, champagne khaki, taupe khaki, beige khaki, or soft neutral khaki shades.

Gentlemen

Barong Tagalog, formal long-sleeves, or formal coat with slacks. Recommended colors are cream khaki, beige khaki, pale khaki, warm taupe, or other soft khaki tones.

Wedding Guests

Formal Khaki Attire

Wedding Guests

Ladies

Formal or semi-formal dresses in the khaki color family. You may wear pale khaki, sand khaki, soft beige khaki, taupe khaki, or ivory khaki.

Gentlemen

Formal long-sleeves, Barong Tagalog, or formal polo with slacks. Please choose khaki-inspired colors such as beige, taupe, cream, pale khaki, or sand khaki.

Strictly No: Jeans, shorts, casual t-shirts, slippers, rubber shoes, overly bright colors, and pure white bridal-looking outfits. Please keep the look formal, soft, and aligned with the khaki wedding motif.
